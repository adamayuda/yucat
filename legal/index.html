Common questions

  • How does scanning work? Take a photo of the product packaging and YuCat identifies the product and produces a nutritional assessment tailored to your cat.
  • Why is an assessment just guidance? Assessments are generated automatically and are informational only — they are not veterinary advice. Always consult your vet about your cat’s diet and health.
  • How do I manage my subscription? Subscriptions are billed through your Apple App Store account. Manage or cancel it in your App Store account settings.
  • How do I delete my data? Email us and we’ll delete or anonymise your data within 30 days.
